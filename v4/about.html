About

A bar with
good taste.

A place worth being seen.

The Spotted James is more than a bar — it's a vibe. Good music, strong drinks, and the kind of crowd that makes every night better than the last.

We built this place for the people who end up in Boulder on a Saturday night — CU students walking down from the Hill, wedding parties in from out of town, Buffs fans heading downtown after Folsom, bachelorettes spending the weekend, travelers looking for the move after dinner on Pearl.

Two blocks off Pearl. Three from campus. Open 'til 2am, every night.

Good people.
Good music. Good times.
