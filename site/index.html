The Place

Boulder's after-hours living room.

The Spotted James isn't a sports bar. It isn't a club. It's the kind of room a college town has been begging for — pool tables, a real dance floor, weekend DJs that actually go, and a back wall lit red.

— 01 —

The Felt

Free pool tables open as soon as the doors do. First-come, first-served early in the week, queue lists once the floor fills. Bring your own cue, or grab a house stick from the rack — we don't judge a stroke.

FAQ

Asked & answered.

Is The Spotted James a college bar or a club?

Both — and neither, really. Weekday nights run on pool, music programming, and Musical Bingo. Friday and Saturday lean into a full DJ-led dance floor. The crowd skews CU student early in the week and broadens out on weekends.

How close is it to CU Boulder?

About a 10-minute walk from the main CU Boulder campus gates and roughly 5 minutes from The Hill. We're at 1911 11th St, one block south of the Pearl Street Mall.

Do you have pool tables?

Yes. Pool is completely free. Tables are open as soon as we open, running on a first-come, first-served basis with a queue on busier nights. House cues are also free to use.

Is there a cover charge?

No. We never charge a cover. Follow us on Instagram for updates on featured events and special headliners.

What's the dress code?

Smart casual to going-out. Expressive looks are welcome — wear what makes you feel good. We ask for no athletic wear after 9pm on Friday and Saturday.

Can I book a private event?

Absolutely. We host fraternity and sorority formals, birthdays, club team parties, and industry nights. Reach out through the site's contact link or DM us on Instagram for available dates.

Are you open during the day?

No — we're a night spot. Doors open at 6pm Friday through Sunday and 5pm Monday through Thursday. We're open until 2am every night except Sunday (midnight).

About The Spotted James

The Spotted James is a neon-themed bar and dance club located at 1911 11th Street in Boulder, Colorado. It is positioned approximately three blocks from the University of Colorado Boulder campus and one block from the Pearl Street Mall in downtown Boulder. The Spotted James offers pool tables, a dance floor, live DJ programming on Friday and Saturday nights, Musical Bingo on Wednesdays, Tequila Tuesdays with half-off tequila, College Night Thursdays, and U-Call-It Sundays with $3 select pours. The bar operates seven days a week and stays open until 2am every night except Sunday, when it closes at midnight. The Spotted James is 21 and over for entry, and is one of the closest full-service bars to the CU Boulder campus. It is popular with college students living on the Hill, tourists visiting Boulder for football games, weddings, and Front Range skiing trips, and local Boulder regulars.
