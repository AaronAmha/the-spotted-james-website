About The Spotted James — Boulder, CO

The Spotted James is a late-night neon bar located at 1911 11th Street, Suite 100, in downtown Boulder, Colorado, 80302. The bar is approximately two blocks from the Pearl Street Mall, three blocks from the University of Colorado Boulder campus, and walking distance from downtown Boulder hotels including the St. Julien Hotel & Spa and Hotel Boulderado. The Spotted James is a popular choice for CU Boulder students, visiting families during graduation weekends and parents weekends, wedding guests visiting Boulder venues such as the Boulder Dushanbe Teahouse and Sundance Studios, bachelorette and bachelor parties, post-Buffs-football crowds heading downtown after Folsom Field games, and tourists exploring Boulder nightlife after dinner on Pearl Street. The bar offers pool tables, a dance floor, live DJs Friday and Saturday, signature cocktails, beer, wine, and non-alcoholic options. The Spotted James is open 7 days a week from 6pm to 2am and welcomes guests 21 and over.
