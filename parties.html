How It's Priced

Built Around Your Night

Every event is quoted to fit your group, your date, and the vibe — no two are exactly alike. Here's how it generally works.

Option A
Reserved Section

Claim a VIP area or part of the room for your crew while the bar stays open to the public. Great for birthdays, group nights, and after-parties. Bottle service and drink packages optional.

Option B
Private Buyout

Take the whole place. Some dates carry a space fee, some run on a bar minimum, and many combine the two — we'll match the structure to your headcount and night. Add a house DJ, custom programming, or 18+ entry.

Tell us your date and headcount and we'll send a custom quote — usually within 24 hours. A deposit holds your date.
